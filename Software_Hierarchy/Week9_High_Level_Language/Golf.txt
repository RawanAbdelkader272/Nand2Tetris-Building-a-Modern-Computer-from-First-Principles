## Golf Simulator

##This project was developed for Week 9 of the Elements of Computing Systems (ESC) class, as part of the Nand2Tetris curriculum.

## Built entirely in the Jack programming language, this application functions as a straightforward digital golf scorecard.

## The program tracks your game by sequentially adding up the strokes from each individual hole.

## Once the round is complete, it tallies the cumulative total and displays the final score to the player.
